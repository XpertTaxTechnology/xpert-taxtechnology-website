GST Services
GST Registration: Assistance with obtaining a unique GST Identification Number (GSTIN).
GST Return Filing: Preparation and submission of various periodic returns, such as GSTR-1 (outward supplies), GSTR-3B (summary return), GSTR-9 (annual return), etc..
GST Payment: Facilitating online tax payments (PMT-06 challan, etc.).
Input Tax Credit (ITC) Management: Advisory and assistance in claiming and managing eligible ITC.
GST Assessment and Audit Support: Services related to self-assessment, provisional assessment, scrutiny of returns, and audits by tax authorities.
E-way Bill & E-invoicing: Generation and management of electronic waybills and invoices for the movement of goods.
Advisory and Consulting: Expert guidance on the applicability of GST rates (e.g., SAC codes for services), compliance requirements, and business restructuring impact.
GST Practitioner (GSTP) Services: Representation before GST authorities by authorized practitioners. 
Income Tax Services
Income Tax Return (ITR) Filing: Preparation and e-filing of ITR for individuals, businesses, and other entities (ITR-1 to ITR-7).
Tax Deduction at Source (TDS) & Tax Collected at Source (TCS) Compliance: Filing of quarterly TDS/TCS returns and issuance of certificates.
PAN Application: Assistance in applying for a Permanent Account Number (PAN).
Tax Planning & Consultancy: Strategic advice to minimize tax liability through eligible deductions (like Section 80C) and exemptions.
Advance Tax Calculation & Payment: Calculating and paying advance tax liability throughout the financial year.
Tax Audit: Auditing accounts for businesses and professionals as required under Section 44AB of the Income Tax Act.
Representation & Litigation Support: Assisting clients during income tax assessments, appeals, and legal proceedings.
Status Tracking: Online viewing of tax credit (Form 26AS/AIS) and refund status.
